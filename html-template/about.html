About

Dr. Molly is a Colorado Springs native who is privileged to serve her hometown at Valor Vision Clinic. As a residency trained optometrist in ocular disease and low vision rehabilitation, her primary focus is enhancing and protecting patients’ most precious sense--sight. She has a passion for caring for patients of all ages and has extensive experience working in medical and surgical based practices and non-profit organizations, co-managing patients with specialists, educators, and occupational therapists. In the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic, Dr. Molly unveiled a need for quality, affordable, transparent, and accessible eye care and founded Valor Vision Clinic LLC. Dr. Molly prides herself on providing the highest quality of care to all patients, regardless of their socioeconomic background, demographics, or ability to pay. In her free time, Dr. Molly enjoys cooking, playing hockey, exploring the outdoors, and spending time with her two cats--Bernard and Harriet.
